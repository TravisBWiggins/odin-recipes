Paleo Chicken Strips

Description

Deliciously gluten free, nut free, and paleo friendly chicken strips made in an oven

Ingredients

  • 3 or more chicken breasts/ 12 or more Chicken wings
  • 1 Bottle of Sake (No you don't need the whole thing!)
  • 2 tbsp Arrowroot flour
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4th tsp pink himalayan salt

Steps

  1. Set an oven to 425 degrees
  2. Take the chicken breasts and place them in a zip-lock bag and fill with sake to cover the chicken and place it in the fridge on a plate or bowl for 30 minutes
  3. Combine the Spices and Arrowroot flour together into a flour blend, this will act as the breading.
  4. Remove the chicken from the bag of sake and pat dry
  5. Slice chicken breasts into strips carefully.
  6. Place the chicken and flour blend into another zip-lock breading and shake it up to coat the chicken
  7. Remove the breaded chicken from the bag and place it on to an oven safe pan
  8. Let Chicken cook for 25 minutes and then serve with a sauce of your choice!
