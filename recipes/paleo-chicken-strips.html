Paleo Chicken Strips

Description

Deliciously gluten free, nut free, and paleo friendly chicken strips made in an oven

Ingredients

  • 3 or more chicken breasts
  • 2 tbsp Arrowroot flour
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/4th tsp pink himalayan salt

Steps

  1. Set an oven to 425 degrees
  2. Combine the Spices and Arrowroot flour together into a flour blend, this will act as the breading.
  3. Slice chicken breasts into strips carefully.
