Veggie Stir Fry

Description

A delicious paleo friendly Vegetable stir fry with no meat needed!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups organic cabbage
  • 1 cup organic carrots sliced
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 cup diced organic onion
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos

Steps

  1. Preheat large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add oil to coat the pan.
  3. Add the carrots and onions and cook for 5 minuntes.
  4. Add cabbage and coconut aminos. Stir to coat all veggies and cook until tender, about 5 minuntes
