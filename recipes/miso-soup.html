Miso Soup

Description

A delicious bowl of miso soup that can be a perfect addition to any meal. This version is made using traditional methods

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of Dashi made with Kombu and Katsuobushi
  • 7 oz of tofu
  • 1 green onion
  • 4 tbsp miso paste
  • 1 tbsp dried wakame seaweed

Steps

  1. Prepare all ingredients by dicing the green onions and setting aside the needed miso paste
  2. Bring dashi to a slow boil
  3. Ladle in the miso paste by stirring inside a strainer or very carefully in a spoon or ladle
  4. Cut tofu into 1.3 cm thick blocks and add to the soup
  5. Add in the wakame seaweed
  6. Right before serving add in the diced green onions to keep aroma and flavor
